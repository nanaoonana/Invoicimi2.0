About Invoicimi

Empowering Ghanaian businesses with simple, affordable invoicing solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Invoicimi was born out of a simple idea: to make professional invoicing accessible to every Ghanaian business owner, regardless of size or technical expertise.

We understand the challenges of running a business in Ghana, from managing cash flow to dealing with late payments. That's why we've built a platform that's tailored specifically for the Ghanaian market.
